Playing Casino Let It Ride Poker

  1. A single 52-card deck is used, consisting of the usual 52 cards.

  2. Player makes three bets of equal size.

  3. Player may pull his first bet back or "let it ride."

  4. Dealer deals the first community card, to be used by the player.

  5. Player may pull his second back or "let it ride."

  6. Dealer deals the second community card, to be used by the player.

  7. Player is paid according to the poker value of his five cards, remaining bets and the posted pay table.

  8. Side bets are usually available, which are explained below.
