Current Winner

Every time a new bet is added to the game, a new winner will be selected based on our provably fair calculation. It indicates who are winner currently. If there is no new bets added until the end of the game, the current winner displayed will be the final winner.

Return Bet Policy

If there is only one player when the lottery drawing occurs, the total bets wagered will be returned to the player.
