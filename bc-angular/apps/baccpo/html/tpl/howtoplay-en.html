Playing Casino Baccpo

  1. Cards are scored as in baccarat. In particular:

  2. Play starts with the player and dealer receiving two cards each. As in blackjack, one dealer card is turned face up and the other face down.

  3. The player shall have the option to stand or take one more card.

  4. After the player has acted, the dealer turns over his other card. Then the dealer takes a third card if he has five or less points, or two threes.

  5. Hand scoring is generally the same as in baccarat, where the score is the terminal digit of the total number of points.

  6. In the event of a tie, the hand with three cards shall outrank a hand with two cards. Otherwise, two hands of the same number of points and cards shall tie.

  7. The highest ranking hand is a 3-card 9 composed of three 3's.

  8. A player 3-3-3 hand shall automatically win, and pay 3 to 1.

  9. Any winning player 7-point hand shall pay 1 to 2.

  10. All other player wins shall pay 1 to 1.

  11. Any 9-point tie, with the same number of cards in both hands, will go to the player. All other ties shall push.

  12. If the dealer has the higher hand, then the player shall lose.
