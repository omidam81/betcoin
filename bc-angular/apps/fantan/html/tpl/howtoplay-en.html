Playing Fan Tan

  • Fan: A bet on a single number. Wins pay 3 to 1 before commission, or 2.85 after.

  • Nim: A bet on two numbers, one of which is indicated as a push. The winning number pays 2 to 1 before commission, or 1.9 after.

  • Kwok: A bet on two numbers, both of which win. The winning number pays 1 to 1 before commission, or 19 to 20 after.

  • Nga Tan: A bet on three numbers, one of which is indicated as a push. The winning numbers pay 1 to 2 before commission, or 19 to 40 after.

  • Sheh-Sam-Hong: A bet on three numbers, all of which win. The winning numbers pay 1 to 3 before commission, or 19 to 60 after.
