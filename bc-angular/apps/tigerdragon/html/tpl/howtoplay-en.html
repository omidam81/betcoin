Playing Tiger Dragon

  1. 8 standard decks are used.
  2. Cards are ranked as in poker, except aces are always low.
  3. A single card is given to Dragon(Dealer) and Tiger(Player) hands.
  4. The primary bets is on which hand will get the higher card.
  5. Other proposition bets are available, as explained below.
