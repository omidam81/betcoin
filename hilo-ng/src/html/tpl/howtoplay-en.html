How to Play

BetCoin ™ HiLo is a classic card game that allows you to wager with the digital currency Bitcoin. It's an easy and fun way to play with and win more Bitcoins!


  1. Login or Register, or play anonymously with your BTC wallet address

  2. Fund your playing balance with Bitcoins using the provided personal deposit address

  3. Select wager amount by using either the slider, percentage of balance buttons, incremental buttons, or by clicking on the wager box. The maximum wager value is adjusted dynamically.

  4. Check your progress and follow players around the world using the in-game Leaderboard and Recent Plays tabs located below the playing screen.

  5. If you play anonymously, your balance will automatically be returned to you after 30 minutes of inactivity or upon closing your browser.

Game Options

  1. Higher: Guess the next card is higher than the previous card

  2. Lower: Guess the next card is lower than the previous card

  3. Black: Guess the next card is in Black color

  4. Red: Guess the next card is in Red color

  5. Combination with both Higher/Lower and Black/Red

Payouts

  1. If the player guesses the next card correctly, the payout amount will be transferred to the account balance immediately, even during the game!

  2. The amount of the payout is dynamically calculated for each option. It is based on the amount of the wager, 1.65% house edge, and the odds of each option at every stage during the game. Basically, the lower odds of the option you choose, the higher the payout will be, and selecting a bet with higher odds of winning has a lower payout.
