How to Play

Keno is one of the most popular lottery games played in modern casinos. BetCoin ™ Keno allows you to wager with the digital currency Bitcoin. It is an easy and fun way to play with and win more Bitcoins!

There are 80 possible numbers out of which 20 are drawn. The object of the game is to correctly guess as many drawn numbers as possible. A player can guess up to 8 numbers per game. The more numbers guessed correctly in a single game the higher the payout.


  1. Login or Register, or play anonymously with your BTC wallet address

  2. Fund your playing balance with Bitcoins using the provided personal deposit address

  3. Select wager amount by using either the slider, percentage of balance buttons, incremental buttons, or by clicking on the wager box. The maximum wager value is adjusted dynamically.

  4. Check your progress and follow players around the world using the in-game Leaderboard and Recent draws tabs located below the playing screen.

  5. If you play anonymously, your balance will automatically be returned to you after 30 minutes of inactivity or upon closing your browser.
